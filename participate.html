Participate In Research

Below is a list of online studies where we are currently collecting data. To participate in this research, click the links below:

What makes a face attractive?

This study is part of a series of experiments investigating factors that influence human facial attractiveness. This survey takes approximately 15 minutes. You can access the survey here.

Men's attitudes towards children.

As part of her MSc dissertation, Ronja Bartlome is conducting a study to identify factors that influence how men interact with children. Participants will be asked to complete several questionnaires and upload a photograph of their face. The study will take approximately 20 minutes. Click here to participate.

Factors that influence judgements on dating apps?

Jess De La Mare is investigating factors that influence attractiveness for her MSc dissertation. Participants will be asked to answer a personality questionnaire and rate short dating profiles for attractiveness. The study takes approx. 20 minutes. Click here to participate.

Attractiveness of people.

For her MSc dissertation, Christeena Satterfield is investigating factors that influence the attractiveness of people. This study takes around 10 minutes. Click here to participate.
